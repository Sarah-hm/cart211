Tiffany Alice Morrison

24 years old, from kahnawake Mohawk Territory

Tiffany Morrison was a 24-year-old mother of one when she went missing from the Kahnawake Mohawk Territory in Quebec in June 2006. The Kahnawake Mohawk Peacekeepers were responsible for the missing persons file. Four years later, the provincial police force for the province took over the investigation when Tiffany’s remains were found in a wooded area near the Honoré Mercier Bridge. The Sûreté du Québec is currently in charge of the homicide investigation, and the family last spoke with police in December 2014.
