FASHION IS A PLAYGROUND

Everyday, we create our own individual fashion brand and narrative. If one does not consider oneself a fashion enthusiast; this is in itself a fashion choice. Wearing solely white t-shirts and a pair of socks with your scandals is a fashion choice. Not a great one, but it is one.

photo credit : @wellsjordon from unspalsh.com

So if you believe

The idea we have been sold of fashion;

The idea that these commercial narratives are the only way to go;

The idea that your own personal fashion can be found within the pages of a Vogue magazine;

Then you have missed the entire point of fashion
